Who are you?

Hello, and welcome to my website! I'm Fif Gallagher, a recent Computer Science graduate from San Francisco State University.

I've always been fascinated with complex systems, even as a kid. Though I didn't know what they were called, I would spend hours making little sorting algorithms for decks of cards. There was real joy in discovering many years later that not only was there a word for the games I would play with myself as a kid, but that there were whole fields of study dedicated to understanding similar ideas!

I intend to carry that joy with me through my career, and try and convey it to others. Maybe by making software that scales really well, distilling complex design requirements into understandable architectures, ensuring code is readable and easily understood by anybody, or by working with others on the beauty of computer science.

Hire Me

    CSC648 class--worked well on a 6-person team, documentation was very presentable, coding standards

    generally passionate about code that's readable, modular, and scalable

In addition to my industry experience, I also have many other experiences that inform my qualities as a worker.
